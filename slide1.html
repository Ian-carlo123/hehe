Who We Are

The Tagolwanen Women Weavers Association is a collective of Indigenous women from Bukidnon who continue the centuries-old tradition of weaving.

Through their craft, they sustain both their livelihood and cultural identity. Each woven mat carries stories of ancestry, resilience, and community strength.

Location: Km. 4 Sayre Highway, Casisang, Malaybalay City, Bukidnon.
Contact: tagolwanenweavers@gmail.com

“Every strand woven is a memory preserved, and every mat is a living archive of our heritage.”
